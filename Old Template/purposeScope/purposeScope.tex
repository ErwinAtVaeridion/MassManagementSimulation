\chapter{Purpose and Scope}
\label{ch:introduction}
The weight status report contained in this document entails the mass properties of all Level 3 (L3) subsystems of the product Breakdown Structure (PBS).
Each L3 subsystem has a basic mass, representing the current best mass estimate, a mass growth allowance and mass uncertainities. 
These mass properties are used to determine the probability of achieving a desired aircraft mass through a statistical simulation.

This document contains all the relevant L3 mass properties, the resulting aircraft mass distribution and the probability of achieving a desired aircraft mass.
